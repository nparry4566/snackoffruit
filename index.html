History of Fruit Snacks

It is believed that fruit snacks derived from the bits of dried fruit that are also a snack. The first form of the fruit snack was the Joray Fruit Rolls. Joray Fruit rolls were created by Louis Shalhoub in the 1970s. They were used by people who needed quick and healthy energy. Now companies like Mott's, Welch's, and Annie's make the modern fruit snack. The modern fruit snack has become more processed, and is now only advertised as healthy. It is no longer as healthy as it used to be. General Mills created the term "fruit snack". The fruit snack businesses is a multimillion-dollar one. It's target audience is children, many of whom love the candy alternate.
